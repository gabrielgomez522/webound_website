Welcome to CARES™

Giving children, adolescents, adults, and families a wide array of life-changing solutions.

Our mission is to help children and adults with mental health concerns and/or developmental disabilities across New York State. We help them obtain the quality care, compassionate support, and comprehensive services they need to achieve their full potential.

At CARES, we create home, community and site based solutions to assist each individual to maximize their abilities, integrate into the community, and achieve sustainable, long-term progress.

Training & Ongoing Support: From Our Community, For Our Community

CARES is empowering the next generation of local community members to deliver life-changing mental health care and support to the people they already know.

At CARES, we believe the best care comes from within the community itself. That’s why we actively train and mentor local individuals to become Direct Support Professionals (DSPs) and Direct Service (DS) workers. Through hands-on training, ongoing support, and real career growth, our team members not only build meaningful careers… but they become a vital source of strength and stability for their neighbors in need. By helping others thrive, they elevate their own lives too. It’s real care, through CARES, that comes full circle.

Want to make a difference in your community? Please contact CARES to learn more about ongoing training and support opportunities for DSP and DS roles.
